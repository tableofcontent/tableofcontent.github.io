If you do not look inward, you will end up looking outward and start comparing yourself to others, blaming others of your own problems, and stop noticing your own flaws, and ultimately you cease to grow.

We mill rice

One of my worst mistake as a business man I made was when I was greedy. It was the year 2011. I bought paddy rice for 15 baht per kilo. The rice price suddenly skyrocketed to 18 baht. At that time we could have profited for at least 15,000,000 baht (426894 USD). But I decided that it’s not the time. I was wishing for it to go up to 20. And guess what? As soon as it reached 18 baht, it fell down to 12. I still feel responsible to this day for the lose of not only 15 million in profits but of another 15 million in lose. The painful lesson I learned here is simple: we mill rice, we don’t trade stocks. When it’s time to make a profit, do. Don’t let your greed fool you into thinking like a broker.
